We didn't just build another bulky sidebar. Every feature is injected seamlessly into YouTube's, JioHotstar's and SonyLIV's native UI so it feels like it was always meant to be there.

🚫

Auto Ad-Skipper (Youtube only)

Say goodbye to the "Skip Ad" button. The extension automatically detects and clicks it for you the exact millisecond it appears. Zero interruption.

⏭️

Smart Double-Tap

Just like on the mobile app, double-click the left or right side of the video player to skip backward or forward with a sleek pulse-merge animation.

🔁

A/B Timeline Looper (Youtube only)

Drag the beautiful yellow handles on the red progress bar to isolate and perfectly loop any specific segment of a video. Perfect for learning music or studying.

📌

Frame-Accurate Bookmarks (Youtube only)

Press the 'B' key to drop a map-pin right on the video timeline. Easily jump back to your favorite moments, visually mapped on the progress bar.

📄

One-Click PDF Export (Youtube only)

Studying a lecture? Instantly generate a clean, print-ready PDF containing screenshots of all your bookmarked frames paired with their subtitle text.

⚙️

Quick Quality Switcher

View and change your video quality instantly with a dedicated button right on the main player bar. No more digging through nested gear menus.
